📖 Babble Tower

Learn Koine Greek by reading the four Gospels one verse at a time.

Read the Gospels in the Original Greek

Babble Tower is a step-by-step Koine Greek learning experience built around the four Gospels: Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John.

Instead of memorizing isolated vocabulary words, you learn Greek through the actual text itself. Each verse becomes a lesson where you learn the words, their meanings, their forms, and their order before moving forward.

📚 Choose Your Gospel

Start with any of the four Gospels and progress chapter by chapter.

  • Matthew
  • Mark
  • Luke
  • John

Select a chapter and begin working through the Greek text one verse at a time.

🔤 Learn Every Word in Context

Every verse is transformed into a personalized vocabulary lesson.

  • Greek word recognition
  • English meaning
  • Word forms
  • Greek word order
  • Vocabulary building through real Scripture

You don't just memorize words — you learn how Greek actually works in real sentences.

🧠 Master the Verse Before Moving On

After studying the vocabulary, you take a quiz using the words from the verse.

  • Review every word from the verse
  • Test your understanding
  • Continue practicing until you achieve 100%

Once you master the verse, Babble Tower unlocks the next verse and your journey continues.

🏛 Learn Ancient Greek Through Ancient Text

Babble Tower is designed for English speakers who want to learn Koine Greek by engaging directly with the language of the New Testament.

Rather than beginning with disconnected vocabulary lists, you build your skills naturally by reading, understanding, and mastering authentic Greek passages.
